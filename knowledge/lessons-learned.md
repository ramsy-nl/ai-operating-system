# Lessons Learned

> Layer 3 — Environment. The team's memory of mistakes, surprises, and hard-won
> insight. The point of writing these down is to **never pay for the same lesson
> twice**. Append a new entry whenever something bites you or surprises you.

## Format

Each lesson: what happened, what we learned, and what to do differently. Keep it
short and actionable — a lesson nobody can act on isn't a lesson.

---

### L-000 — Unverified "done" is the most expensive bug (example)

- **Date:** 2026-06-13
- **What happened:** Work was marked complete based on "it should work," then
  failed in review/production.
- **Lesson:** A completion claim without pasted evidence is a guess.
- **Do differently:** Layer 2 is mandatory — run the check, paste the output,
  then claim done. See `verification/review-checklist.md`.
